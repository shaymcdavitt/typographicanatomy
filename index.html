Typographic
Anatomy Poster.
Created in a typography class, this piece
analyzes the unique and common attributes
of the Bodoni font set. Bodoni became the
font of focus due to its beautiful curved
letterforms and unique thick and thins.
Specifically the letter “T” had been choosen
due to the difference it portrayed between
its lower and uppercase letterforms that really
captures the overall beauty of the font.
During the process, as one letter form was
hung upsidedown, the text began to follow,
this allows the font to better highlight the
attributes that the text is pointing to. This
allows the poster to be viewed right side up
and upside down. Overall being an
informational poster highlighting how this
font uses the typographic elements.
